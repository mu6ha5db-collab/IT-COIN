Welcome to IT Coin

IT Coin (by Ithihad) is a coin for Ithihad members, earned by participating and coordinating programmes. Check your coin status and appeal for any incorrect actions.

Coin Rules

  • Participating in a programme: 2 coins
  • Coordinating a programme: 2 coins
  • Judging a programme: 2 coins
  • First prize: 6 coins
  • Second prize: 4 coins
  • Third prize: 2 coins
  • Spoiling programmes: -5 coins
  • Not following core committee rules: -5 coins
